# Fabless Semiconductor Companies

Fabless companies design semiconductor chips but outsource manufacturing to foundries.

They play a central role in innovation, especially in areas such as artificial intelligence, mobile technologies, and high-performance computing.

---

## Leading Fabless Companies

### NVIDIA

**Focus:** AI, GPUs, data centers

**Role:**

* global leader in AI chips
* dominant in GPUs and data center acceleration

**Strategic Importance:**

* critical for artificial intelligence development
* central to U.S. technological leadership

**Risks:**

* export controls on advanced chips to China
* dependence on external foundries (TSMC)

---

### AMD (Advanced Micro Devices)

**Focus:** CPUs, GPUs, data centers

**Role:**

* major competitor to Intel in CPUs
* strong presence in high-performance computing

**Strategic Importance:**

* diversification of compute supply
* key player in data center infrastructure

**Risks:**

* reliance on TSMC for manufacturing
* competition with Intel and NVIDIA

---

### Qualcomm

**Focus:** mobile processors, 5G

**Role:**

* leader in smartphone chipsets
* key player in wireless communication technologies

**Strategic Importance:**

* essential for global mobile ecosystem
* strong influence in 5G development

**Risks:**

* exposure to smartphone market cycles
* geopolitical tensions affecting global markets

---

### Broadcom

**Focus:** networking, connectivity, enterprise

**Role:**

* major supplier of networking chips
* strong presence in enterprise and cloud infrastructure

**Strategic Importance:**

* critical for data centers and connectivity
* diversified semiconductor portfolio

**Risks:**

* dependency on enterprise demand
* exposure to global economic cycles

---

### MediaTek

**Focus:** mobile SoCs, consumer electronics

**Role:**

* major supplier of smartphone chips (especially mid-range)
* strong presence in Asia markets

**Strategic Importance:**

* key competitor to Qualcomm
* important for supply chain diversification

**Risks:**

* reliance on foundries (TSMC)
* competition in highly competitive mobile market

---

## OSINT Indicators to Monitor

Key signals when analyzing fabless companies:

* chip announcements (new architectures, AI chips)
* partnerships with foundries (TSMC, Samsung)
* export restrictions affecting product markets
* earnings calls and revenue breakdowns
* data center and AI demand trends
* mergers and acquisitions

---

## Strategic Relevance

Fabless companies drive innovation in the semiconductor ecosystem.

They influence:

* AI and computing capabilities
* digital infrastructure
* global technological competition

---

## Key Dependency

Fabless companies depend heavily on:

* advanced foundries (especially TSMC)
* access to cutting-edge manufacturing nodes

This creates structural vulnerabilities in the supply chain.
