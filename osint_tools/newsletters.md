# Semiconductor Newsletters and Monitoring Sources

Regular monitoring is essential for tracking developments in the semiconductor industry, including policy changes, supply chain shifts, and technological trends.

This section compiles key newsletters, think tanks, and recurring publications.

---

## Industry Newsletters

### Semiconductor Industry Association (SIA)

Link: https://www.semiconductors.org/

**Focus:**
- industry trends
- policy developments
- global semiconductor outlook

---

### CSIS (Center for Strategic and International Studies)

Link: https://www.csis.org/

**Focus:**
- semiconductor geopolitics
- export controls
- U.S.–China tech competition

---

### Asia in Review

Link: https://asiareview.cpg-online.de/

**Focus:**
- political and economic developments in Asia
- semiconductor and tech policy in East Asia

---

### Stratechery

Link: https://stratechery.com/

**Focus:**
- technology business strategy
- big tech and semiconductor dynamics

---

### FT – Technology & Commodities

Link: https://www.ft.com/

**Focus:**
- global tech industry
- supply chains
- critical materials

---

### Reuters – Technology & Supply Chains

Link: https://www.reuters.com/

**Focus:**
- breaking news
- industrial developments
- geopolitical risks

---

## Think Tanks & Research Centers

- CSIS (U.S.)
- MERICS (Germany)
- Brookings Institution
- Peterson Institute for International Economics

---

## What to Monitor

Key topics:

- export control announcements
- fab investments and expansion
- mergers and acquisitions
- supply chain disruptions
- policy and subsidy programs
- technological breakthroughs (AI chips, nodes)

---

## Analytical Use

These sources allow analysts to:

- maintain situational awareness
- detect early signals of change
- contextualize trade and supply chain data
- track policy developments in real time
